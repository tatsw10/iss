Coach

Born and raised in the vibrant landscapes of São Tomé e Príncipe, Izidro Sousa's journey is a testament to his unwavering dedication to personal and professional growth. At the age of 18, driven by a passion for technology, he made a pivotal move to Portugal, specifically to Minho, where he delved into the world of Management Informatics. Izidro's early years were marked by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and skill development. His academic journey paved the way for a dynamic career in Lisbon, where he honed his expertise in IT project management. As he navigated the tech landscape, Izidro emerged as a visionary leader, ultimately assuming the role of Head of the Tech Department at Mailtech. Beyond his technical prowess, Izidro Sousa is a seasoned Executive MBA graduate, bringing a strategic edge to his leadership. His commitment to holistic development led him to delve into the realms of coaching and training, specializing in speaking leadership. Today, Izidro not only manages complex IT projects but also shares his insights and empowers others as a coach and trainer. In the ever-evolving tech industry, Izidro Sousa stands as a beacon of leadership, seamlessly blending technical expertise with a passion for cultivating the next generation of effective communicators and leaders. His multifaceted journey embodies the spirit of continuous learning and innovation, inspiring those around him to reach new heights.

Izidro Sousa's journey is a testament to the transformative power of conquering personal fears. Battling a deep-seated fear of public speaking, he decided to confront this challenge head-on and joined Toastmasters. In the supportive and empowering environment of Toastmasters, Izidro not only overcame his fear but flourished to become the Toastmasters Europe champion. Having faced and conquered his own anxieties, Izidro is now passionate about helping others navigate the often daunting path of public speaking. As a seasoned Toastmasters coach, he shares his insights and techniques to inspire confidence and effective communication. Izidro's story serves as a living example that anyone can transform from a hesitant speaker to a champion, given the right support and dedication. Beyond his role as Head of the Tech Department at Mailtech, Izidro Sousa dedicates his time to paying it forward, helping individuals discover their voice and thrive in public speaking. His commitment to empowering others underscores the idea that triumph over fear is not just a personal victory but an opportunity to uplift those who are on a similar journey.

Toastmasters

TedX

Inspired by his own growth and recognizing the profound impact of effective communication, he seized an opportunity to share his insights on a larger stage. Toastmasters opened the door for him to present a TEDx talk, where he passionately spoke about the importance of integrating public speaking into school curricula. In his TEDx presentation, Izidro emphasized the transformative power of learning public speaking skills early in life. Drawing from personal experience, he underscored how such skills not only build confidence but also empower individuals to navigate the challenges of the modern world. His engaging talk resonated with the audience, sparking a conversation about the vital role public speaking education plays in shaping the future leaders of tomorrow.
